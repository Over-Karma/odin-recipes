Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a fiber packed grain that can help you stay feeling full throughout the day. Oatmeal goes well with many different things and can be made to suite different types of taste.

A pot, some milk, and oatmeal are all thats required for this dish.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Milk
  • (optional) Topping

Steps

  1. pour a healty amount of oatmeal into a pot
  2. add about a cup a milk
  3. add heat
  4. boil until milk and oatmeal become a congealed mush
  5. (optional) add toppings of choice
