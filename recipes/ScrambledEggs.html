Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled Eggs are a classic breakfest dish that can give you the nutrition you need to start the day, with the added caveat that they taste great! This recipe is great for cooking beginners and only requires two ingredients and a pan to make.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Seasonings (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Prepare a clean pan for use
    2. take out desired amount of eggs
    3. prepare necessarry butter for use
    4. heat your pan on your stove
    5. place a lump of butter on heated pan and completely melt it; The melted butter should be enough for the entire pan
    6. place select amount of eggs in buttered pan
    7. stir cooking eggs as they cook and take breaks between each stir
    8. remove scrambled eggs from pan when they reach desired firmness and texture
    9. (optional step) add preffered seasoning
