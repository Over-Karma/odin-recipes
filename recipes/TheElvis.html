The Elvis

Description

A style of sandwhich made famous by it's association with Elvis Presley.

This unusual variation of a PB&J adds some twist that are sure to keep you interested.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Peanut Butter
  • Bananas
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. cook your bacon using your preferred method (pan fry is reccomended)
  2. prepare bread slices used for sandwhich
  3. apply peanut butter on bread in select manner
  4. place sliced bananas and cooked bacon on peanut buttered bread
  5. cover slice of bread with other bread
  6. butter a pan and heat it
  7. place sandwhich in heated pan and toast till golden on both sides
